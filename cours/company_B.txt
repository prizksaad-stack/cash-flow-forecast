BlueEnergy Ltd reduced its CO₂ emissions by 27% between 2020 and 2024 by investing $320 million in renewable energy infrastructure.
The company’s operations are now powered by 65% renewable electricity, and its supply chain audits cover 95% of direct suppliers.
BlueEnergy publishes a verified annual sustainability report reviewed by an independent auditor, following the GRI standards.
The firm has set measurable goals to achieve net-zero emissions by 2035, and progress is reported quarterly on its investor portal.
